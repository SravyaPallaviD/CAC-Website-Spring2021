Local Agencies Collaborate to Assist Child Abuse Victims
November 1, 2020

Murfreesboro, TN – “19 days feels like a very short period of time to an adult,” stated Child Advocacy Center Director Sharon De Boer. “But, to a child being abused every day, 19 days can feel like an eternity.” From November 1-19, the Child Advocacy Center is partnering with 16 local agencies to increase awareness about the plight of child abuse victims and encourage adults to react responsibly and report child abuse.
