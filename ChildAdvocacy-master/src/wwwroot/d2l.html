Additional Information

Darkness to Light is a nationwide initiative designed to train adults how to protect children by teaching them the 5 Steps to Protecting Children from Child Sexual Abuse.

In 2012, the Child Advocacy Center set a goal to train 11,575 adults, which represents 5% of the adult population of Rutherford and Cannon Counties. The Child Advocacy Center exceeded this goal in 2020 and set a new goal to train 10% of the adult population of Rutherford and Cannon Counties.

Step 1:
LEARN THE FACTS

1 in 10 children are sexually abused. 90% of them know their abuser.

Step 2:
MINIMIZE OPPORTUNITY

Eliminate or reduce isolated, one-on-one situations to decrease risk for abuse.

Step 3:
TALK ABOUT IT

Have open conversations with children about our bodies, sexual abuse, and boundaries.

Step 4:
RECOGNIZE THE SIGNS

Know the signs of abuse to protect children from further harm.

Step 5:
REACT RESPONSIBLY

Respond to risky behaviors and act on suspicions.
